## ðŸ“ Recording Tips

### Equipment Needed:
- Screen recording software (OBS, QuickTime, or Loom)
- Microphone (built-in is fine)
- Quiet environment

### Best Practices:
1. **Practice** the script 2-3 times before recording
2. **Keep it concise** - aim for 75 seconds
3. **Show, don't tell** - let visuals do the work
4. **Speak clearly** at a moderate pace
5. **Edit out** mistakes and long pauses

### Screen Setup:
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or 1280x720
- **Browser**: Full-screen, hide bookmarks bar
- **Terminal**: Clean, large font
- **Close** unrelated tabs and notifications
